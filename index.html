Who we are

SATA is a non-profit organization made up of staff and volunteers striving to preserve and sustain our community.

Founded in 2009 by local trail enthusiets, SATA aims to provide health enhancing recreational opportunities for all trail users while preserving and improving our local green spaces.

Over the last 5 years, we have successfully created more than 12 miles of trail in the Salem area. We remain dedicated to providing support and creating trails.
